UCD-AUT Math Bio Seminar Series

This is seminar series on topics related to mathematical biology. This is a broad topic which covers mathematical neuroscience, mathematical epidemiology, and any other topic that seems relevant. This is an online seminar series hosted by coordinators Dr Indranil Ghosh and Dr Hammed Olawale Fatoyinbo. Keep an eye out for more news: speaker and date announcements.

For any other information please contact the eitherIndra or Hammed.
