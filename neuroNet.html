Accelerating network simulations of slow-fast denatured Morris-Lecar neurons using Python.

This blog could have been an email! However, I decided to take the longer road of explicitly breaking down each and every step I took to run simulations of a network of slow-fast neurons. This blog is intended to teach the community how to accelerate simulations of neuron models on connected random networks.
